Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Trauma Informed ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our iPhone application.

No Data Collection Beyond Your Device

Trauma Informed operates entirely on your device and does not collect or transmit your personal data to external servers.

  • All personal data, including journal entries, worksheet responses, meditation history, and app settings, is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not use cloud services, remote servers, or any other technology that would transmit your data beyond your device.
  • The app functions completely offline, with no data transmitted over the internet.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following types of data locally on your device:

  1. Mood Journal Entries: Text entries and mood ratings you create within the app.
  2. CBT Worksheet Responses: Your responses to cognitive behavioral therapy exercises.
  3. Meditation Progress: Records of your meditation sessions and progress.
  4. Implementation Intentions: Any intentions you set within the app.
  5. App Settings and Preferences: Your personalized settings within the app.
  6. Reading Progress: Your progress through the PDF guides included in the app.

No Third-Party Sharing

  • We do not share any of your data with third parties.
  • No analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies are implemented in our app.
  • Your data remains completely private and under your control.

Permissions Requested

Our app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Calendar Access (Optional): Only requested if you choose to add implementation intentions to your calendar. Used solely for creating calendar events at your request.

No other system permissions are requested or used.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over its retention and deletion:

  • To delete individual items: Use the delete functionality within the relevant section of the app.
  • To delete all app data: Uninstall the app from your device.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through an app update notification.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. All data storage is local to the device, and we implement no tracking, profiling, or data collection mechanisms.

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device and under your control, you maintain all rights to this data. We cannot access, alter, or delete any of your data, as it never leaves your device.

Compliance with Regulations

This app is designed to comply with privacy regulations including:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • App Store Guidelines for Privacy

Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data beyond your device, many provisions of these regulations that concern data collection and processing do not apply to our app.

Technical Implementation Details

Permissions Used

Calendar (NSCalendarsUsageDescription)

Used only when a user explicitly chooses to add implementation intentions to their calendar. The app will request permission only at the time this feature is used.

Privacy Declarations

Privacy Manifest

Our app includes a comprehensive privacy manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) that transparently declares all API access and confirms no data collection.

APIs and Frameworks Used

SwiftUI

Used for the user interface architecture.

PDFKit

Used to display PDF resources included with the app.

AVFoundation

Used for audio playback in meditation sessions.

EventKit

Used when user opts to add implementation intentions to calendar.

Combine

Used for reactive programming patterns in the app.

UserDefaults

Used to store user preferences and progress data.

Data Structures and Storage

Local File Storage

The app stores user-generated content, including:

  • Mood journal entries stored as structured data in local files
  • CBT worksheet responses saved in the app's private documents directory
  • Progress tracking data saved using UserDefaults
  • Pre-loaded content such as PDFs and audio resources included in the app bundle

Data Models

The app uses Swift structures for:

  • MoodEntry: Tracking mood journals with timestamps, content, and mood ratings
  • CBTWorksheet: Storing worksheet responses with questions and answers
  • MeditationSettings: Storing meditation preferences and session history
  • ImplementationIntention: Storing user-created implementation intentions

Local-Only Implementation

All data persistence is implemented using:

  • UserDefaults for app settings and progress tracking
  • Local file system for user-created content
  • In-memory state management during app usage

No database connections, API endpoints, or network requests are made at any point.
